Prizza Mike's Insane 180sx Revival

Prizza Mike faced every enthusiasts nightmare when a driver ran a red light and t-boned his prized 240sx.

Rather than accepting to have the car be totalled by insurance, Mike took the harder route and decided to rebuilt his car. After multiple years and countless hours of Frankensteining the car with donor parts from other cars the once mangled 240sx is now back on the road. The project is a testamint of the incredible community built around Silvias.

Mike now coninutes his passion and is saving other cars who's lives were seemingly over, never leaving a car for dead. His current projects include a Nissan Gloria that had significant rear-end dammage and a Nissan 300zx swapped with a 240sx K25de engine.
